**AI Thinking Mode** is an artistic interpretation and conceptual visualization of what artificial intelligence might "look like" when it's processing information. This data sculpture presents AI cognition as a living, breathing entity with measurable dimensions and dynamic properties.

### ğŸŒ The Visualization

- **3D Neural Globe**: An animated sphere with dynamic shader effects representing the AI's "brain"

  - **Cognitive Signature**: Unique AI session identifiers
  - **Model Information**: Gemini 2.5 Pro integration
